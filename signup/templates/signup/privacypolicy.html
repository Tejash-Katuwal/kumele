Kumele Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2025

Welcome to Kumele! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and PayPal account details (if you connect your PayPal account).
  • Event Data: Information about events you create or join, including event details and payment information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, such as IP addresses and browser details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services, such as facilitating event creation and payments.
  • Process payments through third-party providers like PayPal.
  • Communicate with you about your account and events.
  • Ensure the security of our platform.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Third-Party Payment Providers: We share necessary data with PayPal to process payments and payouts.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, we may share your data to comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Security

We use encryption and other security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns.
